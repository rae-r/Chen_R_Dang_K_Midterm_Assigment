Dubbed Anime VS Subbed Anime

Why subbed anime is better than dubbed:

  • Original voice acting for original experience and culture
  • Content won't be cut for dubbing purposes (Censoring, cutting scenes)
  • Good if you're learning the language and need to know how it is spoken (slang and formal)
  • Japanese voice actors are more emotional and feel more in-character
  • More accessible and more available varied content
  • Better for recent release and on release watching, can take a while for the English dub to release.
